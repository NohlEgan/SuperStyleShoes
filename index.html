MEET THE TEAM

Nohl Egan

Founder

Nohl started Super Style Shoes in his garage when he was only 25 years old. He had no idea that it would quickly turn into such a raging success! He mainly attributes the success of his business to the shoes, because “stylish shoes just sell themselves, you know?”

Art Vandelay

CEO

Art Vandelay has been the CEO of Super Style shoes for about 4 years. Previously a leader in the latex industry, Art Vandelay decided to jump ship to the shoe industry after seeing the rising success of Super Style Shoes. When asked if he ever regrets his decision of leaving the latex industry for Super Style Shoes, Art simply laughs and shakes his head.

Kel Varnsen

President

Kel Varnsen began working with Nohl Egan only about 1 year after Super Style Shoes was founded. He was incredibly confident in the business, as he saw e-commerce shoes retailers to be the way of the future. Needless to say, his confidence has paid off!
